Hi, my name is Chris!

Web developer in training

Hi, my name is Chris, I am 27 years old and originally from Hollywood, FL. I moved to the Charlotte area in 2008 and graduated from Belmont Abbey College in 2014 with Business/Marketing Bachelor's degree. I worked as an office assistant where I interened at Southern Advantage, Co. and then was promoted to shipping manager. After a period of time though, I decided it was time for a change and that I wanted to follow a career path that truly interested me. This is when I decided to look into coding and eventually made the decision to join the UNCC Coding Bootcamp. I hope to excell in the program and eventually land a job in web development where I hope to begin a long and productive career!

My interests are history, music, art, video games, movies and current events. My hobbies include going to concerts, art galleries, hiking, practicing yoga, reading, and I hope to eventually start mountain biking. My main hobby, however is travel and one of my goals in life is to eventually travel most of the world.
